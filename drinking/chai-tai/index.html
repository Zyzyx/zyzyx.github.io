2 min read
Rum Savory Winter

Cocktail

A spiced, cozy seasonal take on the exotic Trader Vic classic.

  • 1 ½ oz Chai-infused Rum*
  • ½ oz Amaro Nonino
  • ½ oz Dry Curaçao**
  • ½ oz Almond Orgeat
  • 1 oz lime juice

Add all ingredients to shaker, add ice, shake vigorously until properly chilled. Double-strain over crushed ice in a rocks glass. Garnish with a sprig of fresh mint, taking care to squeeze the leaves in your hand before placement in order to release their fragrant oils.

*Recommended: Appleton Estate 12 Year **Recommended: Pierre Ferrand, Marie Brizard

Chai-infused Rum

  • 750ml bottle of Appleton Estate 12 Year Rum
  • 2 tbsp of loose leaf Masala Chai tea

Combine all ingredients in a quart-sized Mason jar; reserve bottle. Let infuse for 2 hours at room temperature. Strain back into original bottle and label.

Almond Orgeat

  • 1 cup peeled, blanched Almonds
  • hot (not boiling) water
  • 4 cups white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ oz Orange Blossom Water
  • 1 oz vodka (to fortify)

Put the almonds in a quart-sized Mason jar and fill with hot water. Let macerate for one hour, then drain the water out, reserving the nuts. Put the nuts in a blender with the sugar, salt, and 4 cups hot water. Blend on high for one minute. Fine-strain through a nut bag or cheesecloth (a cloth napkin works in a pinch), pressing to extract all of the liquid. Add the vodka and Orange Blossom Water to the strained mixture, stirring in to mix. Store in a clean container and keep refrigerated. Should keep for one month.
