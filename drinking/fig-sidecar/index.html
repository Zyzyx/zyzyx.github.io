Brandy Savory Winter

Cocktail

A rich, improved-upon version of the classic that will pair perfectly with the spirit of the Holidays.

  • 1 ½ oz Fig-infused Cognac*
  • ½ oz Cinnamon-infused 80-proof Orange Liqueur**
  • ½ oz Pedro Ximenez Sherry***
  • ¾ oz lemon juice

Add all ingredients to shaker, add ice, shake vigorously until properly chilled. Double-strain into a chilled coupe glass and garnish with an orange peel, taking care to express the citrus oil over the edge and stem of the glass, and across the surface of the drink.

*Recommended: Courvoisier VS **Recommended: Cointreau ***Recommended: Emilio Hidalgo, El Candado

Cinnamon-infused Orange Liqueur

  • 12 cinnamon sticks
  • 1L bottle 80 - proof Orange Liqueur

Divide the cinnamon sticks into two equal groups and crack. Place the broken sticks into two quart-sized Mason jars and mix with a 1L bottle of spirit, split evenly between the two jars. Shake. Let sit at room temperature for three days, agitating daily. Fine-strain and re-bottle.

Fig-infused Cognac

  • 15 dried mission figs
  • 1L bottle Cognac

Slice the figs into halves and combine with Cognac in a clean container for three days, agitating daily. Fine-strain and re-bottle.
