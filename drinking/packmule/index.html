Cocktail

A spicy, smoky, loaded take on the Mule.

  • 2 oz Mezcal*
  • ¾ oz Grilled Pineapple Cordial
  • ½ oz lime juice
  • 15 dashes (15 ml) Habanero Tincture
  • 2 ½ oz Ginger Beer**

Add all ingredients except Ginger Beer to shaker, add ice, shake vigorously until properly chilled. Add Ginger Beer to the shaken cocktail. Double-strain over new ice in copper or tin mule mug. Garnish with a lime wheel perched on top of the drink, sprinkled with some Cayenne Salt (equal parts kosher salt and cayenne pepper)

*Recommended: Del Maguey Vida, El Silencio, Creyente Joven, Banhez Joven Espadín y Barril **Recommended: Fever Tree, Q Mixers

Grilled Pineapple Cordial

  • 1 medium-sized ripe pineapple
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp citric acid
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 oz ‘white’ rum (to fortify)

Skin pineapple and cut into planks. Grill evenly on two sides, about four minutes on each side. Chop the grilled planks into cubes, then combine with all remaining ingredients in a bowl and let macerate for 2 hours (with salt and sugar covering everything). Blend with an immersion blender and then fine strain, pressing against the blend to extract all the liquid. Store in a clean container and keep refrigerated. Should keep for one month.

Habanero Tincture

  • 1 cup blanco tequila
  • 4 habanero peppers, sliced and stemmed

Add habanero peppers to tequila in a non-reactive container. Let steep for two days. Fine-strain, then store in bitters dasher-style bottle.
