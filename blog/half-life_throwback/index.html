Half-Life Throwback

2012-09-03

Sans-Serif just got a new computer, and I spent Labor Day playing Half Life 2 mods, so I figured a post about the best of them was in order.

The first one I cannot recommend enough is Mission Improbable. It’s a few hours long, looks fantastic, and easily mistaken for a professional project; it requires Episode 2.

The second was Minerva: Metastasis (see link at the bottom) which was also great, but had a few glitches to note: the waves surrounding the island were a little “off”, and I had a few crashes in the second half of the first chapter.

I figured both of these would be a good prelude to the Half Life 1 remake done with the Half 2 engine, which is due September 14th.
