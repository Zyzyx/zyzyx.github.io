Hotdogs

Apr 24, 2023
2 min read

Need some ideas for hotdogs? Here a list from around the nation and other parts of the world.

New York

  • Hotdog in a plain white bun
  • Add mustard, sauerkraut and an onion sauce (or just onions)

Seattle

  • Bratwurst in a long white bun
  • Add cream cheese, slice jalapenos, chopped cabbage, and sriracha

Sonora

  • Bacon wrapped hotdog in a split soft roll
  • Add relish, tomatoes, onions, avocado chunks, nad mayonnaise

Tijuana

  • Bacon wrapped hotdog in a soft/squishy white roll
  • Grilled bell peppers, onions, and jalapenos

Dodger

  • Footlong dog in a footlong bun
  • Ketchup, mustard, onions, relish

Doyer

  • Footlong dog in a footlong bun
  • Add mustard, nacho cheese, pickled jalapenos, and salas

Colombia

  • Hotdog in a plain white bun
  • Add ketchup, mustard, mayo, pineapple, crushed potato chips, and a boiled quail egg speared on top

Carolina

  • Hotdog in a plain white bun
  • Add chili, chopped onions, and cole slaw

Montreal

  • “All-dressed” New England bun
  • Steamed hotdog
  • Add mustard, chopped onions, and cabbage

Michigan

  • Hotdog in a plain white bun
  • Add tomato-based meat chili sauce, and mustard

Italian

  • Deep fried hotdog
  • Italian roll
  • Add bell peppers, onions, french fries, ketchup, and mustard

Coney

  • Short hotdog in a short bun
  • Add chili, chopped onions, and shredded cheddar

Chicago

  • Hotdog in a poppy seed bun
  • Add a pickle spear, celery salt, tomatoes, pickled peppers, chopped onions, green relish, and mustard

Kansas City

  • Hotdog in a sesame seed bun
  • Add sauerkraut, and melted swiss cheese

Brazil

  • Hotdog in a split roll
  • Add pico de gallo, corn, grated parmesan cheese, shredded carrots, diced ham, cilantro, and shoestring fries

Chile

  • Hotdog in a plain white bun
  • Add chopped tomatoes, sauerkraut, mashed avocado, and mayo

Guatemala

  • Bacon wrapped hotdog on a corn tortilla
  • Smear avocado on the tortilla
  • Add shredded lettuce, cabbage, mayo, and chopped onions

Denmark

  • Long hotdog in a normal bun
  • Add pickle chips, remoulade, ketchup, mustard, fried onions, and chopped onions

Sweden

  • Hot surrounded by mashed potatoes, shrimp salad, lettuce, fried onions
  • Rolled in a wrap

Baltimore

  • Fried bologna wrapped around a fried hotdog in a squishy bun
  • Add mustard

Denver

  • Hotdog in a plain white bun
  • Add chopped red onion, green chile sauce, sour cream, and chopped jalapenos

Memphis

  • Bacon-wrapped hotdog in a plain white bun
  • Add BBQ sauce, chopped scallions, and shredded cheddar

Georgia

  • Open-faced hotdog bun
  • Sliced hotdog
  • Add cheese, chili, chopped onions, sliced pickles, oyster crackers, ketchup and mustard

Argentina

  • Chorizo link in a soft hero roll
  • Add chimichurri, tomato mix, and pickled red onion

Cleveland

  • Kielbasa in a bun
  • Add french fries, hot sauce, and cole slaw
