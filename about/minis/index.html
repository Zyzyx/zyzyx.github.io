Minis

Apr 15, 2023
2 min read

For fun, I paint miniature models that I think look cool. I don’t play war games like Warhammer or anything like that, just paint. I do play a lot of board games and often paint minis in those, or the minis in friends’ board games. Random minis to paint make for good birthday presents or work rewards too. I’m not too concerned with having a presentable “set”.

A long time ago I dipped my toe into photography and had bought a macro lens for my camera, which is a Canon Rebel T5i. That’s been gathering dust for a while, and this page seemed like a good reason to dust it off.
